How it Started

From a Home Kitchen to Your Doorstep

It all started in a small home kitchen in Hyderabad, where our founder Rajesh Kumar would cook for his neighbours on weekends. The response was overwhelming — everyone kept asking for more.

In 2020, Rajesh launched Kilocurry with a focus on bulk, fresh, affordable curries. The name comes from the idea that the best food is always ordered by the kilo — because you always want more!

Today we serve 500+ customers daily from a FSSAI-certified kitchen with a passionate team of 15 chefs and delivery partners.
